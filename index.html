Interests

Padel

Padel, sometimes called Padel Tennis, is a racket sport typically played in doubles on an enclosed court slightly smaller than a doubles tennis court. Although Padel shares the same scoring system as tennis, the rules, strokes, and technique are different but similar at the same time. The balls used are similar but with a little less pressure. The main differences are that the court has walls and the balls can be played off them in a similar way as in the game of squash and that solid, stringless bats are used.

I love playing Padel as it is a very competitive sport, where you need to be able to rapidly communicate visually or verbally with your teammate in order to win. It's a very good form of exercise a can be very benefitial for your health while having tons of fun at the same time.

Martial Arts

Wing Chun Kung Fu

Wing Chun is a martial art with origins from China, it's purpose is to be able to protect your self especially from larger and more powerful opponents. The art of Wing Chun teaches you to be calm and use others' force against them to gain the advantage. Wing Chun is the martial art Bruce Lee originally trained for. Perhaps you have heard of it from the movie Yip Man, or from Robert Downey Junior.

Perfect way to train both your mind and your body. The training was hard and was targeting to make your core body stronger since balance is of most importance. And all of this while learning how to protect yourself.

Kalah Combat System

Kalah is a modern combat system targeting in street defence. It was tricky and unpredictable just as street fight really is. Tried it while studying in my BSc but closed due to Covid and then I relocated. Would love to continue my training some day.
